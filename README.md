# Brain Dump
**Write fast. Don't stop.**

Brain Dump is a writing tool that helps you write without stopping. If you stop typing for too long, you lose your work. This helps you get your thoughts down quickly.

## How it works
The app has different modes to make sure you keep writing:

- **Shrink Mode**: The writing area gets smaller when you stop typing. If it gets too small, the session ends.
- **Erase Mode**: Your words start to disappear one by one if you take a break.
- **Strictness**: You can choose how much time you have to think (Forgiving, Standard, or Brutal).

## Benefits
- **Overcome Writer's Block**: The pressure forces you to write something instead of staring at a blank page.
- **Boost Productivity**: You finish drafts much faster when you aren't allowed to stop.
- **Focus Better**: The high stakes keep you focused on your work.
- **Get Raw Ideas Down**: It helps you capture your first thoughts before you try to edit them.

## Features
- **Word Count**: See how many words you have written.
- **Timer**: track how long you have been writing.
- **Stats**: See your words per minute and how many times you almost lost.
- **Simple Design**: A dark, clean look to help you focus.

## How to use
1. Install the app with `npm install`.
2. Start the app with `npm run dev`.
3. Open `http://localhost:3000` in your browser.
4. Type your topic and click **Start Session**.
5. Keep typing until you are done!
